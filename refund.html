Refund Policy

Last Updated: April 7, 2026

1. Commitment to Excellence

At MaadBuka, we strive to deliver the highest quality gourmet experiences. If your order does not meet our rigorous standards, we are committed to making it right.

2. Order Cancellations

Orders can be cancelled with a full refund within 5 minutes of placement. After this window, prepation by our artisan chefs has begun, and cancellation may result in a partial or no refund.

3. Reporting Issues

Any issues regarding food quality, presentation, or missing items must be reported within 2 hours of delivery. Please provide photographic evidence via our concierge service for immediate review.

4. Refund Processing

Once approved, refunds are processed back to the original payment method within 3-5 business days. In some cases, we may offer immediate gourmet credits for your next elite dining experience.

5. Dietary Requirements

While we take every precaution, MaadBuka cannot be held liable for allergic reactions unless a specific dietary requirement was clearly communicated and confirmed at the time of order.
